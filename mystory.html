Dual Oneness

Space. A planet. With an iron-based core causing two differentiated magnetic poles. A race of travelers. Powerful energetic beings capable of creating matter out of thin air. Just by thought. Separate beings yet part of one grand consciousness. Once, this energetic beings, while visiting different stars systems and planets, found themselves *trapped* in this earthy magnetic planet from which they will have to thrive in order to get out of and continue their journey. This new adaptation of them is a fleshy biological machine, as advanced as themselves in energy form. Yet they've forgotten who they were. They know nothing of their powers. They are starting to learn how operate this machines. And that's us. xD
